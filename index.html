Export Process & FAQ

Simple steps to get your order delivered.

How do I place a bulk order?

Simply send us your excel sheet with OEM numbers or Part names via the contact form or WhatsApp. Our team will generate a Proforma Invoice within 24 hours.

What are your payment terms?

We usually work on 30% Advance and 70% against the Bill of Lading (BL) copy. For smaller air shipments, we require 100% advance.

Do you provide certificates of origin?

Yes, we provide all necessary documentation including Certificate of Origin (COO), Commercial Invoice, and Packing List for smooth customs clearance.
